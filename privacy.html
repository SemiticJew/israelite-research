Our Commitment

Semitic Jew Inc is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation organized in the United States. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and personal information of our supporters, readers, donors, and community members. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you interact with our website or services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, mailing address, and donation-related information. We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, and usage data to improve website functionality and user experience.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to respond to inquiries, process donations, provide updates regarding our mission, maintain organizational records, and improve the quality of our educational and research services. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Donation Processing

Donations to Semitic Jew Inc may be processed through secure third-party payment processors, including Stripe and Zeffy. These processors maintain their own privacy and security policies. Semitic Jew Inc does not store full credit card information on its servers.

Email Communications

If you contact us through our official email channels or subscribe to updates, your information may be used to respond to inquiries or send organizational communications. You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our Website may link to third-party websites or integrate services provided by external platforms. These services operate under their own privacy policies. Semitic Jew Inc is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services.

Cookies

The Website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather limited analytics information. Users may adjust browser settings to disable cookies if desired.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we maintain about you, subject to applicable legal requirements. Requests may be submitted through our official contact channels.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect organizational changes or legal developments. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact Us

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed through our official contact page at semiticJew.org/contact.html.
