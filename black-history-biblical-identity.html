Myths This Page Will Help Correct

These claims are common, but they must be tested instead of repeated.

  • Africa had no civilization before Europe.
  • Slavery existed everywhere, so American slavery was nothing unique.
  • Africans sold slaves, so Europeans bear no special responsibility.
  • Christianity civilized Black people.
  • Race is neutral and disconnected from power.
  • Biblical identity has no historical or material consequences.

Suggested Study Path

Move from Scripture to history, then from history to correction and practice.

  • Start with Scripture: study Israel, captivity, exile, empire, judgment, and restoration in the Tanakh and Apocrypha.
  • Map the world: use the global map to compare biblical regions with historical and scholarly notes.
  • Test the myths: use logic to identify deflection, false equivalence, special pleading, and inherited assumptions.
  • Rebuild identity: connect truth to discipline, household order, community responsibility, and obedience.
