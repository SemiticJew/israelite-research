This catechism is a concise Q&A summary. It is not exhaustive, but a faithful baseline for teaching and examination.

Q1

What is our highest aim?

To love YHWH our God with all our heart, soul, mind, and strength; and our neighbor as ourselves.

Q2

What is Scripture?

The inspired, sufficient, and trustworthy Word of God, preserved and profitable for teaching, reproof, correction, and training in righteousness.

Q3

Who are the Israelites?

The covenant people descended from Jacob, called to be a light to the nations and fulfilled in Messiah’s work among all peoples.

Q4

What is our hope?

Resurrection and the restoration of all things under the reign of the Most High.

Our distinctives simply clarify emphasis and practice — they do not replace the core confession of the faith.
