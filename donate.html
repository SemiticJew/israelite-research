Support the Work

Your support helps Semitic Jew Inc produce biblical research, educational articles, Bible-study tools, media teaching, and public learning resources rooted in Scripture, logic, and truth.

Why Support Semitic Jew Inc?

Semitic Jew Inc is a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit serving the public through biblical research, digital publishing, media education, Bible-study tools, and teaching resources. Donations help keep this work accessible and strengthen the organization’s ability to produce consistent public education.

  • Produce Scripture-centered articles, studies, and media teachings.
  • Maintain and improve semiticjew.org as a public educational resource.
  • Develop Bible-study tools, research aids, and teaching materials.
  • Support lectures, public education, and future instructional programming.
Biblical Research Supports research, writing, editing, documentation, and publication of educational articles and studies.
Media Education Supports video, podcast, and teaching content that explains Scripture, logic, history, and public issues.
Study Tools Supports Bible-study resources, digital tools, reader improvements, and public learning materials.
Public Access Supports website hosting, accessibility improvements, outreach, and the continued availability of free resources.
Semitic Jew Inc is organized for educational and public-benefit purposes. Contributions support the organization’s mission and programming.
Transparency: Gifts support educational programming, website operations, research, media production, and teaching resources. Donation receipts and records are provided through the giving platform when available.
