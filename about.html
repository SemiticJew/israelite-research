About the Organization

Semitic Jew Inc

Semitic Jew Inc is an educational nonprofit dedicated to advancing biblical literacy, historical understanding, and critical thinking through research, digital media, publications, and instructional programming.

Mission

Our mission is to advance biblical literacy, historical understanding, and critical thinking through research, publications, digital media, instructional programming, and public education.

Scripture

We promote careful engagement with biblical texts, context, language, and interpretation.

Logic

We encourage sound reasoning, careful definitions, and consistent analysis.

Truth

We develop educational resources that examine Scripture, history, and evidence with clarity.

History and Work

Semitic Jew Inc was organized to support educational work centered on biblical literacy, historical research, and public instruction. The organization produces articles, Bible-study resources, teaching materials, digital media, and other educational tools designed to help readers and viewers engage Scripture and history with discipline and clarity.

Current projects include SemiticJew.org, biblical research articles, Bible-study resources, educational media production, lectures, podcast and video teaching, and digital tools for studying Scripture, history, and logic.
