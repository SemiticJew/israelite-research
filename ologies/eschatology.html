Eschatology

Eschatology (Gk, ἔσχατος + λογία, “eschatos + logia,” discourse about last things); hope of Israel’s restoration, the reign of the King from Zion, resurrection, judgment, and the renewal of creation—with blessing ordered through Israel to the nations.
