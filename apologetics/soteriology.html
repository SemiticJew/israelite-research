Soteriology

Soteriology (Gk, σωτηρία + λογία, “sōtēria + logia,” the study/discourse about salvation); study of deliverance as covenant faithfulness—sacrifice, redemption, and restoration of Israel with blessing extended to the nations, without erasing Israel’s identity and order.
