Ecclesiology

Ecclesiology (Gk, ἐκκλησία + λογία, “ekklēsia + logia,” discourse about the assembly); study of the set-apart assembly of Israel and grafted participants—its identity, order, leadership, unity, and mission—while preserving Israel’s covenant roles and Shemitic-Hamitic heritage.
