Bible App Roadmap

  • Foundation Use the current Biblia system, Scripture data, chapter reader, lexicon presentation, and cross-reference behavior.
  • Study Memory Add local bookmarks, continue reading, recently opened chapters, and saved notes.
  • Offline Layer Cache reader shells, Scripture data, and key study pages for app-like reading when connection is limited.
  • Native Path Once the PWA proves useful, package the same experience for App Store and Google Play consideration.
