About the Organization

Semitic Jew Inc

Semitic Jew Inc is a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit advancing biblical literacy, historical understanding, and critical thinking through research, digital media, public education, and teaching resources.

Mission

Our mission is to advance biblical literacy, historical understanding, and critical thinking through research, publications, digital media, instructional programming, and public education. We create public resources that help readers and viewers study Scripture, evaluate arguments, and engage history with discipline and clarity.

Scripture

We promote careful engagement with biblical texts, context, language, and interpretation.

Logic

We encourage sound reasoning, careful definitions, and consistent analysis.

Truth

We develop educational resources that examine Scripture, history, and evidence with clarity.

History and Work

Semitic Jew Inc was organized to support educational work centered on biblical literacy, historical research, and public instruction. The organization produces articles, Bible-study resources, teaching materials, digital media, and other educational tools designed to help readers and viewers engage Scripture and history with discipline and clarity.

Current projects include SemiticJew.org, biblical research articles, Bible-study resources, educational media production, lectures, podcast and video teaching, and digital tools for studying Scripture, history, and logic.

Public Educational Work

Semitic Jew Inc serves the public through free and accessible educational resources. Our work includes biblical research, articles, digital media, Bible-study tools, teaching resources, and future curriculum development.

Biblical Research

Scripture-centered articles, historical analysis, apologetics, and research-based teaching.

Media Education

Podcast, video, and public teaching content that explains Scripture, logic, and history.

Bible-Study Tools

Digital tools, Biblia resources, study aids, and reader improvements for public use.

Teaching Resources

Instructional materials, lectures, future courses, and public learning resources.
