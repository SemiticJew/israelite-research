Privacy Policy

This page explains how Semitic Jew Inc handles website information, contact communication, analytics, donations, and third-party services.

Overview

Semitic Jew Inc respects the privacy of visitors who use semiticjew.org. This policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and how third-party tools may interact with the website.

If you contact Semitic Jew Inc by email or through linked services, you may voluntarily provide information such as your name, email address, organization, message content, or inquiry details.

This site may use Google Analytics or related Google tools to understand general website activity, such as pages visited, approximate traffic patterns, device/browser information, and engagement trends. This information helps improve the website and educational content.

Donations may be processed through third-party platforms such as Zeffy. Payment and donation information submitted through those platforms is governed by the privacy policies and terms of the third-party provider.

Cookies and Tracking

The website or third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies to operate, measure traffic, improve performance, and support analytics.

Email Communication

If you email Semitic Jew Inc, your message may be retained for communication, recordkeeping, administrative response, media inquiry handling, or organization-related follow-up.

Information Sharing

Semitic Jew Inc does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the organization, respond to inquiries, comply with legal obligations, protect the website, or use trusted service providers.

External Links

This website may link to YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Zeffy, Google services, social media platforms, or other third-party websites. Semitic Jew Inc is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to contact@semiticjew.org.
