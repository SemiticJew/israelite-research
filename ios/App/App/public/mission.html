Mission of Semitic Jew Inc

Mission

Semitic Jew Inc exists to advance biblical literacy, historical understanding, and critical thinking through research, public education, digital media, Bible-study tools, and teaching resources.

Our Mission

Semitic Jew Inc produces educational resources rooted in Scripture, logic, and historical analysis so the public can study biblical texts, test arguments, and engage history with clarity.

The organization serves readers, viewers, students, families, teachers, churches, study groups, and independent learners who want disciplined biblical education and public-facing study resources.

Our work is designed to make research, articles, media teaching, Bible-study tools, and instructional resources accessible to the public through semiticjew.org and related educational platforms.

Scripture We promote careful engagement with biblical texts, context, language, law, prophecy, and interpretation.
Logic We encourage sound reasoning, clear definitions, valid argumentation, and disciplined analysis.
Truth We develop educational resources that examine Scripture, history, and evidence with clarity and seriousness.

Who We Serve

Semitic Jew Inc serves the public through free and accessible educational content. The work is not limited to one classroom, one platform, or one format.

Public Learners Individuals seeking biblical literacy, historical context, and structured study resources.
Students and Families Learners who need accessible teaching, careful definitions, and clear educational materials.
Researchers and Teachers People looking for organized articles, references, public teaching, and study tools.
Media Audiences Viewers and listeners engaging biblical topics, logic, culture, history, and public issues.

Mission in Practice

The mission is carried out through public educational resources and accessible study materials.

  • Biblical research and educational articles.
  • Media teaching through video, podcast, and public commentary.
  • Bible-study tools, reader resources, and study aids.
  • Instructional materials, lectures, and future curriculum development.
  • Public education rooted in Scripture, logic, history, and truth.
