Public Educational Programs

Programs

Semitic Jew Inc provides public educational programs through biblical research, media education, Bible-study tools, teaching resources, and instructional content rooted in Scripture, logic, and historical analysis.

What We Do

Semitic Jew Inc is a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit. The organization develops and publishes resources that help the public study Scripture, understand biblical history, evaluate arguments, and access educational media.

These programs are delivered through semiticjew.org, articles, media teaching, public study tools, digital resources, and future instructional programming.

Program Area 01

Biblical Research & Articles

We publish research-driven articles and studies that examine Scripture, biblical context, logic, history, apologetics, and public questions.

  • Scripture-centered articles and studies.
  • Historical and contextual analysis.
  • Apologetics, logic, and argument evaluation.
  • Public education through written resources.
