Is Truth Reliable?

If our truth changes with our feelings, it cannot guide our lives. Scripture says truth must be anchored in our knowledge of God’s unchanging character through His trustworthy Word. The question is not whether truth exists, but whether we will receive it through sound teaching and faith.

God Is Truthful

“God is not a man, that he should lie” (Numbers 23:19); he “cannot lie” (Titus 1:2). Because God is faithful and unchanging, what He reveals can be relied upon in every generation (Malachi 3:6).

God’s Word Is Truth

Jesus prays, “Sanctify them through Thy truth: Thy word is truth” (John 17:17). Every word of God is pure and proven (Proverbs 30:5); “the sum of Thy word is truth” (Psalm 119:160). Scripture is God-breathed and therefore reliable for teaching and correction (2 Timothy 3:16).

Truth Reveals and Reorients

Truth exposes deception and gives insight to help us navigate life’s biggest questions. The prophet declares that God’s word stands forever (Isaiah 40:8). To walk in truth is to walk in the light, not in the shifting fog of opinion (Psalm 86:11).

Truth is reliable because God is reliable. We do not build a faith out of uncertainty; we build a faith through the soundness and validity of God.

For believers, trusting Scripture does not mean ignoring the hard questions; it means bringing our questions to the One whose words have never failed. As we learn to hear and obey, truth will steady our minds and anchor our lives.
