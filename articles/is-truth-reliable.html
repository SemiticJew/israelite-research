Is Truth Reliable?

Semitic Jew • Article

A. God is Truth — the Source through Logic, Nature, and Revelation

God is the ultimate source of truth (John 17:17; Ps 119:160). Logic reflects His consistency, nature bears witness (Rom 1:20), and Scripture reveals His will (2 Tim 3:16).

B. Satan is A Deceiver — Distorting Truth

Satan’s weapon is deception (John 8:44; Gen 3:1). He distorts Scripture (Mt 4:6), promotes fallacies (2 Pet 3:16), and leads false teachers (2 Cor 11:14).

C. Satan Deceives the Earth — Nations and Propaganda

The dragon deceives the whole world (Rev 12:9; Rev 20:3). Nations spread propaganda (Dan 8:12), embrace false teaching (2 Tim 4:3), and hide Israel’s true identity (Jer 6:16; Rev 18:23).
