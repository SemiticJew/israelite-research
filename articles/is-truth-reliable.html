Is Truth Reliable?

Darriell L. Dickerson
Semitic Jew — Essays in Scripture & Reason
X

This essay asks a straightforward question: is truth reliable? Our answer is anchored in three claims that frame a biblical and logical understanding of reality.

A. God is Truth — and what proceeds from Him and His people is truthful

In Scripture, God is not merely a speaker of truth; He is its source and ground...

“Sanctify them through thy truth: thy word is truth.” John 17:17

B. The devil is the father of lies — and what proceeds from him and his people is deception

In Scripture, falsehood is not neutral error; it is moral rebellion...

“When he speaketh a lie, he speaketh of his own: for he is a liar, and the father of it.” John 8:44

C. Restoring what deception obscures — the dignity and history of Israel

One powerful deception is historical: the enemy has labored to sever the public memory...

“Truth is fallen in the street, and equity cannot enter.” Isaiah 59:14

Conclusion

Truth is reliable because God is reliable...
